# ATS Trivia Questions

## 🔴 Set 1: AMD General Questions

**Q1. When was AMD (Advanced Micro Devices) founded?**

* A) January 15, 1965
* B) May 1, 1969
* C) March 22, 1971
* D) June 10, 1975

> ✅ **Answer: B** — AMD was founded on May 1, 1969, by Jerry Sanders and colleagues from Fairchild Semiconductor. [\[en.wikipedia.org\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AMD)

***

**Q2. Who is the current Chair and CEO of AMD?**

* A) Jerry Sanders III
* B) Pat Gelsinger
* C) Dr. Lisa Su
* D) Mark Papermaster

> ✅ **Answer: C** — Dr. Lisa Su has been AMD's CEO since 2014 and currently serves as Chair and CEO. [\[britannica.com\]](https://www.britannica.com/money/Advanced-Micro-Devices-Inc)

***

**Q3. Where is AMD's corporate headquarters located?**

* A) Austin, Texas
* B) Sunnyvale, California
* C) Santa Clara, California
* D) San Jose, California

> ✅ **Answer: C** — AMD is headquartered at 2485 Augustine Drive, Santa Clara, California. [\[amd.com\]](https://www.amd.com/en/corporate.html)

***

**Q4. Which company did AMD acquire in 2022 to expand into FPGA and adaptive SoC products?**

* A) Altera
* B) Lattice Semiconductor
* C) Xilinx
* D) Microchip Technology

> ✅ **Answer: C** — AMD completed its acquisition of Xilinx in 2022, adding FPGA and adaptive SoC products (Versal, Virtex, Kintex families) to its portfolio. [\[en.wikipedia.org\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AMD)

***

**Q5. What is AMD's ticker symbol on the NASDAQ exchange?**

* A) AMDS
* B) ADM
* C) AMD
* D) ADMI

> ✅ **Answer: C** — AMD trades on NASDAQ under the ticker symbol **AMD**. [\[financecharts.com\]](https://www.financecharts.com/stocks/AMD/profile)

***

## 🟢 Set 2: Product Related Questions

**Q1. What is the codename for AMD's next-generation EPYC server processor based on the Zen 6 architecture?**

* A) Turin
* B) Genoa
* C) Venice
* D) Bergamo

> ✅ **Answer: C** — EPYC "Venice" is built on the Zen 6 architecture using TSMC's 2nm process, scaling up to 256 cores per package. **Q2. What is AMD "Helios"?**

* A) A consumer gaming GPU
* B) An open rack-scale AI platform
* C) A laptop processor series
* D) A cloud software service

> ✅ **Answer: B** — "Helios" is AMD's rack-scale AI platform, powered by Instinct MI455X GPUs, EPYC "Venice" CPUs, and Pensando "Vulcano" NICs. **Q3. How much AI performance can a single AMD "Helios" rack deliver?**

* A) Up to 500 petaFLOPS
* B) Up to 1.4 exaFLOPS
* C) Up to 2.9 exaFLOPS
* D) Up to 10.0 exaFLOPS

> ✅ **Answer: C** — A single Helios rack delivers up to **2.9 exaFLOPS** of FP4 performance with 18 compute trays, each containing four MI455X GPUs. * A) Google DeepMind

**Q5. At CES 2026, what desktop processor did AMD announce as "the fastest gaming processor"?**

* A) Ryzen 9 9950X
* B) Ryzen 7 9800X3D
